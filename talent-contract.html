Talent Contract

This Talent Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on , by and between Los Angels Entertainment, LLC, DBA PLUSH ("Agency") and ("Model"). This Agreement outlines the terms and conditions under which the Model will provide services to the Agency.

Scope of Agreement

The Agency agrees to hire the Model, and the Model agrees to provide services such as modeling, dancing, or any other relevant services for the Agency, as requested and agreed upon. The Model will participate in events, photoshoots, promotional campaigns, performances, and other assignments as assigned by the Agency.

Term of Agreement

The term of this Agreement will begin on and will remain in effect until unless terminated earlier as per the terms provided in this Agreement.

Exclusivity

The Model agrees to work exclusively with the Agency for the duration of this Agreement. During this time, the Model shall not engage in any modeling, promotional work, or other services for any other company, agency, or individual without prior written consent from the Agency's management. A breach of this exclusivity clause may result in the immediate termination of this Agreement, legal action for any resulting damages, or other financial penalties.

Usage Rights & Content Ownership

a. The Agency shall have the exclusive right to use, reproduce, distribute, display, or license any photographs, videos, media, or other content created during the Model's assignments for commercial, promotional, or other purposes, including online and print media.

b. The Model agrees that all content produced for the Agency, including photos, videos, recordings, or live performances, is the sole property of the Agency, and the Model waives any rights to ownership of this content.

c. The Model agrees that they will not use, distribute, or display any content produced for the Agency without prior written consent from the Agency.

Model Obligations

The Model agrees to:

  • Provide professional services as required, including modeling, dancing, influencing, atmosphere modeling, and production modeling;
  • Maintain professional conduct at all events, performances, or assignments;
  • Adhere to all instructions provided by the Agency, including those related to safety, appearance, and behavior standards;
  • Ensure that their physical appearance, including hair, makeup, wardrobe, and hygiene, meets the Agency’s standards.

Agency Obligations

The Agency agrees to:

  • Provide the Model with reasonable notice of assignments, including details about location, time, and performance requirements;
  • Provide safe and professional environments for all assignments;
  • Ensure all promotional material is appropriate and aligned with the Model's agreed-upon terms of service.

Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

The Model agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary, sensitive, or confidential information related to the Agency, its clients, partners, and projects during and after the term of this Agreement. This includes, but is not limited to, information related to the Agency's business practices, client lists, project details, and any private events attended by the Model.

Behavior and Conduct

The Model acknowledges that they are expected to behave in a manner that positively reflects on the Agency during all assignments and events. Any unprofessional or inappropriate behavior, including but not limited to intoxication, failure to adhere to the dress code, or conduct that violates public decency laws, may result in immediate termination of this Agreement without further payment obligations by the Agency.

Termination

a. Either party may terminate this Agreement with written notice.

b. The Agency may terminate the Agreement immediately if the Model fails to meet professional standards, violates any clause, or engages in conduct that could harm the Agency's reputation.

c. Upon termination, the Agency retains ownership of all content created during the course of the agreement.

Independent Contractor Status

The Model is an independent contractor and not an employee of the Agency. The Model shall be responsible for all taxes, insurance, and other financial obligations related to their earnings. The Model acknowledges that they are not entitled to any benefits provided to employees, including health insurance, worker's compensation, or retirement plans.

Liability and Waiver

The Model agrees to release the Agency from any liability related to injuries, accidents, or incidents that may occur during performances, photoshoots, or other assignments unless such incidents are caused by gross negligence by the Agency. The Model also waives any claims to compensation beyond what is explicitly discussed on a per-gig basis prior to each assignment.

Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of San Andreas. Any disputes arising from this Agreement will be resolved in the courts of San Andreas.

Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and representations, whether written or oral. No modifications to this Agreement shall be valid unless in writing and signed by both parties.
